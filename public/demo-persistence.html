Free vs Pro — how data persists
Free tier (localStorage): Tasks survive closing the tab and coming back the next day on the same browser. If the user clears browser data, tasks are gone.

Pro tier (Firestore sync): Tasks are written to the cloud in real time. Open on your phone, your laptop, or a fresh browser — they're all there. Even if you never re-open the tab, a background sync keeps everything up to date.
