A voice-generated task list

We created Poplist because too much energy gets wasted before the doing even begins — organising, categorising, prioritising, trying to remember everything. That invisible effort is exhausting.

Poplist is designed to take that weight off. No need to worry about forgetting, no need to tidy your thoughts before you speak, and no need to carry unfinished tasks in your head. Less mental load. More action.

FAQ

What is Poplist?

An instant, voice-generated task list for thoughts that pop into your mind.

Who is Poplist for?

Busy minds, people on the go, and curious people who always have something bubbling up.

How does it work?

Talk out what's on your mind. Poplist catches the thought and organises it into a list.

Do I need to organise my thoughts first?

No. Just let it out. Poplist does the organising.

What can I use it for?

Reminders, errands, ideas, tasks, random “don’t let me forget this” moments — all of it.

Why is it called Poplist?

Because thoughts pop up. Poplist helps you catch them before they disappear.

Is my data safe?

Your voice and tasks are processed to organise your list, and stored securely in your account. We don’t sell or share your personal data. You can delete your data anytime from Settings.

Will my data be used to train AI?

Your personal task data is not used to train AI models. We use AI to help organise what you say into a list, but your content stays yours.

Is it free?

You can use Poplist for free with up to 10 tasks. For more features like colour schemes and personalised categories, consider PRO.
