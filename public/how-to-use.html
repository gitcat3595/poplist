How to use
Poplist

Talk, tap, done. Your AI-powered to-do list that thinks like you.

Getting started
1
Tap the mic and speak
Say everything on your mind in one go — groceries, meetings, errands. Poplist listens and sorts it all automatically.
Try: "Buy milk, email John, gym tomorrow"
2
AI sorts your tasks
Poplist understands what each task is about and puts it in the right category — Work, Home, Personal, or Other. It also decides if it's for Today, This Week, or Later.
3
Tap to complete
Done with a task? Tap it. It flies off with a satisfying animation. When everything's done, you get a celebration screen — you earned it.
4
Filter to focus
Switch between Today, This Week, and Later views. Or filter by category — only see Work tasks, or just Home. You're in control.
5
Export any time
Copy your list to clipboard, export as CSV for spreadsheets, or grab a plain-text version to paste anywhere.
Key features
🎤
Voice input
Speak naturally in English or Japanese. No special commands — just talk like you normally would.
🤖
AI categorisation
Every task is automatically placed into Work, Home, Personal, or Other. Timing is assigned too — Today, This Week, or Later.
📋
Bulk add (Brain Dump)
Paste a wall of text and Poplist extracts and sorts every task automatically.
🎨
Colour themes PRO
Choose from 13 accent colours. Change the whole app's look to match your mood or workspace.
🔵
Category colours PRO
Set a different colour for each category — Work in blue, Home in green, Personal in purple.
🔑
Custom keywords PRO
Teach the AI your vocabulary. Map your own words to categories so the AI learns how you think.
🔁
Task rollover PRO
Unfinished tasks automatically carry forward to the next day. Never lose track of what's left.
📊
Weekly stats PRO
See how productive you've been this week, broken down by category. PRO shows full history.
☁️
Cloud sync PRO
Sign in and your tasks sync across all your devices instantly. Pick up where you left off anywhere.
